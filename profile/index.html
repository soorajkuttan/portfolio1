About

"The biggest risk is not taking any risk."

Mark Zuckerberg is a visionary entrepreneur, software engineer, and philanthropist best known as the co-founder and CEO of Meta Platforms, formerly Facebook. Launching Facebook in 2004 from his Harvard dorm room, he revolutionized global communication, shaping the digital landscape with social media, AI, and virtual reality innovations. Under his leadership, Meta expanded to own major platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp while pioneering advancements in artificial intelligence and the metaverse. Beyond technology, he co-founded the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, committing billions to philanthropy in education, healthcare, and scientific research. His mission is to create a more interconnected world through immersive technology and AI-driven solutions.

Projects

FACEBOOK

Founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004, Facebook is a global social media platform that revolutionized digital communication. It enables users to connect, share, and interact through posts, messages, and multimedia content. Over the years, Facebook expanded its services, including Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp, shaping the future of social networking.

Metaverse & Virtual Reality

Mark Zuckerberg envisions the Metaverse as the future of digital interaction, powered by Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR). His goal is to create an immersive, interconnected virtual world where people can work, socialize, and explore beyond physical boundaries. Through Meta (formerly Facebook), he is developing advanced VR technologies like Horizon Worlds and Quest headsets, aiming to revolutionize communication and digital experiences.

Meta (AI) & (ML)

Meta leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to enhance user experiences across its platforms. AI powers content recommendations, chatbots, speech recognition, and image processing, while ML helps improve personalized feeds, moderation, and virtual assistants. Meta is also developing advanced AI models for the Metaverse, enabling realistic avatars, natural language processing, and immersive VR interactions.

Internet Connectivity

Mark Zuckerberg has been actively working to improve global internet connectivity through initiatives like Internet.org and Free Basics. His goal is to provide affordable internet access to remote and underserved areas, enabling people to connect, learn, and grow digitally. Meta has also explored satellites, drones, and AI-driven solutions to expand internet reach worldwide.

Awards

Forbes Billionaire

Mark Zuckerberg, the co-founder of Facebook and CEO of Meta, is one of the world's richest people, according to Forbes. In January 2025, he was ranked third on Forbes' list of the world's billionaires, with a net worth of $211.8 billion.

Time magazine's of the Year

TIME magazine has chosen 26-year old Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg as its Person of the Year for 2010. Since 1920 TIME has chosen a person, or a group of people, who have changed the world for the better , or sometimes for worse.

Harvard Honorary Degree (2017)

Mark Zuckerberg did not graduate from Harvard. He attended the university as an undergraduate but dropped out. Mr. Zuckerberg received an honorary doctorate from Harvard in 2017
